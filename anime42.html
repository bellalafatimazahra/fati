Spy x Family

Spy × Family (stylized as SPY×FAMILY and pronounced "spy family")[2][3][4] is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuya Endo. The story follows a spy who has to "build a family" to execute a mission, not realizing that his adopted daughter is a telepath, and the woman he agrees to marry is a skilled assassin.
