Pokemon

Pokémon[a] is a Japanese media franchise consisting of video games, animated series and films, a trading card game, and other related media. The franchise takes place in a shared universe in which humans co-exist with creatures known as Pokémon, a large variety of species endowed with special powers . The franchise's target audience is children aged 5 to 12,[1] but it is known to attract people of all ages
