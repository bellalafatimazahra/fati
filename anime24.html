Gleipnir

Gleipnir (Japanese: グレイプニル, Hepburn: Gureipuniru) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Sun Takeda. It was serialized in Kodansha's seinen manga magazine Young Magazine the 3rd from October 2015 to April 2021, transferred to Monthly Young Magazine, where it ran from May 2021 to April 2023. Its chapters have been collected in fourteen tankōbon volumes as of July 2023.
