YEmiya-san Chi no Kyou no Gohan

Today's Menu for the Emiya Family (衛宮さんちの今日のごはん, Emiya-sanchi no Kyō no Gohan) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by TAa. The series has been serialized on Kadokawa Shoten's Young Ace Up website since January 26, 2016, and has been collected in nine tankōbon volumes as of November 25, 2023. The manga is licensed in North America by Denpa. They released its first English volume on March 13, 2019.
